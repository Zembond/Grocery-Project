Here's what these people do for you

From the beginning, FreshDirect has set out to provide something different. We are a community partner with a mission to deliver the highest quality and service that customers can find. This is an environment in which associates can innovate, grow and have successful careers: most of our leaders have come up through our ranks. Thanks to our associates' extraordinary efforts, we are the northeast's leading online fresh food grocer. I hope you'll take the next step and advance your career with us.

What Our Team Says

Candice

Senior Transportation Manager

One of the things I appreciate about working at FreshDirect is how the company consistently keeps evolving. I'm empowered to bring my ideas and be a part of the growth of the business. I started in 2009 as a route driver. Since then, I have been promoted to field manager, operations manager, assistant site manager and site manager. I became a senior transportation manager in August 2019. I love being a part of a hard-working, talented team with a strong entrepreneurial spirit.

Floyd

Depot Lead

I came to FreshDirect nine years ago with several years of truck driving experience. I started as a route driver and was promoted to a depot lead after one year. I have moved to three different depots in the New York City area and have mentored new employees. A large part of my job is helping our runners manage their routes and ensuring our customers receive their orders on time. I really enjoy the people I work with and the opportunity to earn a good living to help support my family.

Johanna

Meat Master Butcher

Working hard at FreshDirect has opened lots of new opportunities for me. I started seven years ago in the sortation department and from there was transferred to other departments, including meat. I was accepted into the meat apprenticeship program and upon graduating was promoted to meat butcher. You can be very successful at FreshDirect if you are willing to work hard and are open to learning new skills.
